Interpretation
--------------
The dimerisation and local spin-correlation cases were run at matched V_BD_eff = 0.02. In this static phenomenological model, the Hamiltonian depends on delta and C1 only through V_BD_eff.
Their direct spectrum difference is therefore the decisive control: relative max difference = 2.623e-17.
Conclusion: Step 2 separates constant mixing from enhanced effective mixing, but it does not separate the microscopic origin of the enhanced mixing when delta and C1 only enter through the same scalar V_BD_eff.
To distinguish phonon/dimerisation physics from local spin-correlation physics, the next model must add a mechanism that changes more than the static scalar mixing, for example a dynamic phonon coordinate, a k-sector dependence, a separate lineshape/dephasing channel, or distinct T/B trends.
